Interaction Flow Design: Serious Game Development

This project was commissioned to bring an older learning game into the future for a larger corporate audience. Not only did the game get a face-lift, but a wealth of new exciting features and functionality. I was integrated into the beginning of this project to develop a concise & straightforward interaction design for the game as well as prepare basic prototypes to demonstrate gameplay to our business partners.
