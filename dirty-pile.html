Form Design:
Mobile Laundry Service

Background

In a brief meeting with the business owner, I was able to gather all of the requirements needed for this laundry service order form. In the meeting, we discussed his list desired fields & the requirement of each, service options & pricing, and payment options.

The Challenge

Although small in theory, forms can be some of the hardest interfaces to design for the best user experience. Based on the meeting with the client, there were a number of required fields that needed to be filled out. My goal was to create a form including these fields that was not frustrating for the user and easy to manipulate for both desktop and mobile. I also wanted to make all charges and pricing clear as the user filled out their request and give the user some sense of control in their preferences.

The Result

Due to the length of this form, I first divided the form into sections of relevant information. I included styled radio buttons instead of the default to add some visual appeal to the form. I also added a calculation aspect to the form to give clear visibility into the cost of the service.
